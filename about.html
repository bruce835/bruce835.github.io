About

I really started getting into game development with my still-unfinished bullet-hell
project, Pseudo-Zealous. Will likely start devlogging it once I actually lock in.
It has not been my primary focus recently, but I plan to finish it by the end of 2025.
The graphical side of the project has been taken over by my little brother. I am also
solo-developing(for now, anyways)a text-based adventure rpg called Slay The Orc King(STOK).

I have also produced a bare-bones app to automate posting to this website. Affecionately
called "cppost"(a concatenation of C++, the language it is written in, and 'post'"), it
currently only supports h3, img, and p tags, and only p tags can be styled from
within the program, but I will expand it eventually. This, as well as STOK, are completely
open-source, avaiable on my github.
